About me

Hi. My name is Kamil Kielan and I am web a designer. I've created my very first website back in the end of 90' when to access internet you had to listen to a little tune first. Many things has changed since that moment and my site containg only links on a white background wouldn't impress anyone today. I follow everything what is fresh and groundbreaking and implement that in my work. Unlike 90% websites on the internet that are fixed all my web projects are build to be responsive . They adapt to any mobile or desktop device so a user's experience is always the best possible.
